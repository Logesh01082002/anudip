VisionPLUS -- is a suite of software modules, primarily used by banks and financial 
               institutions, for managing credit card, debit card, prepaid card, 
               and loan products. 
card pac -- Product Approval Committee (PAC) This committee is a key decision-making body

             products, including various card types (credit cards, debit cards, purchasing
              cards, etc.).
